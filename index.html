Mitchell Fernando

Multi-disciplinary Designer. I work with clients to build systems and memorable experiences. With a background in Communication Studies, Linguistics, and Japanese Studies, I am interested in how language and culture interact to influence our understanding of the world.

Also, as a former teacher, I like to approach design in ways that turn ideas to experiences that people can easily interact with, understand, and most importantly enjoy.
